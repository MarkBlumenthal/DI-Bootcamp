About Me

I was born in Johannesburg, South Africa. After High School I decided
to go and study Travel & Tourism. After the 3 year course, I decided
to Move to sunny Cape Town where I lived for almost 15 years. I took
up a job in Property Management where I was very happy for 8 years.
Living in Cape Town, allowed me to persue my side passion of fishing.
I love the outdoors and try to spend as much time in nature as possible.
After moving to Israel with my wife, I decided it was the perfect oppertunity
to start a new career in the High Tech industry. I find tremendous joy in
programming and building web applications & websites. The idea that
I can be apart of building a website or app that thousands of people use
gives me alot of pride and joy.
